\section{Research Questions}

The experiments address three research questions.

\begin{enumerate}
  \item \textbf{How do monolingual baselines and cross-lingual transfer routes compare?} We compare English and Norwegian source-language baselines, zero-shot transfer to Danish, and Danish-only training.
  \item \textbf{How much performance is lost in zero-shot transfer, and how much is recovered by Danish fine-tuning?} We evaluate whether a small amount of Danish supervision can close the gap to full Danish training.
  \item \textbf{Does lower linguistic distance to Danish align with better transfer?} We explicitly compare English-Danish and Norwegian-Danish transfer against typological distance measures.
\end{enumerate}

The third question is the most important follow-up to the proposal. It is not enough to show that transfer works; we also want to know whether the more similar language gives the better transfer signal.
